Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really no per-user pricing?

That's right. Kango charges per organization, not per user. Add your entire team — project managers, superintendents, office staff — without worrying about per-seat costs.

What's included in the free trial?

Everything. You get full access to every module during your trial period. No features are locked or limited.

Can I import data from other platforms?

Yes. Kango supports MS Project import for schedules, CSV import for contacts, and we can assist with data migration from other construction management platforms.

How does QuickBooks integration work?

Connect your QuickBooks Online account and sync vendors, customers, invoices, and payments bidirectionally. Changes in either system stay in sync automatically.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted in transit with TLS, hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure (Vercel + Neon PostgreSQL), and access is controlled by role-based permissions.

Can subcontractors access the system?

Subcontractors and consultants can view shared content through public share links — no account needed. For full access, you can invite them as users at no additional cost.
