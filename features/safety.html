Safety Management

Inspections, incident reports, toolbox talks, observations, and training records — build a culture of safety with documentation that's always audit-ready.

Safety Inspections

Schedule and conduct safety inspections with customizable checklists. Document findings with photos, assign corrective actions, and track resolution. All inspection data is searchable and exportable.

  • Customizable inspection checklists
  • Photo documentation
  • Corrective action tracking
  • Exportable reports
Safety Inspection

Incident Reporting & OSHA Tracking

Document incidents with detailed reports including witness statements, photos, and root cause analysis. Track OSHA recordable incidents and maintain your TRIR and DART rates automatically.

  • OSHA recordable tracking
  • Witness statement capture
  • Root cause analysis
  • Automatic TRIR/DART calculation
Incident Report

Toolbox Talk Management

Schedule and document toolbox talks with attendance tracking. Choose from a library of safety topics or create your own. Generate sign-in sheets and maintain a complete training record.

  • Topic library
  • Attendance tracking
  • Digital sign-in sheets
  • Training record generation
Toolbox Talk

Safety Observations

Empower your team to report both safe and at-risk behaviors. Track observations by category, location, and trade. Use the data to identify trends and focus your safety efforts where they matter most.

  • Safe and at-risk behavior tracking
  • Category and trend analysis
  • Location-based reporting
  • Proactive safety insights
Safety Observation

Training & Certification Tracking

Track employee certifications, training completions, and expiration dates. Get alerts before certifications expire so your team is always compliant.

  • Certification tracking
  • Expiration alerts
  • Training history log
  • Compliance reporting
