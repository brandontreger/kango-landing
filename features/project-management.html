Project Management

Custom-built Gantt charts, cross-module calendar, RFI tracking, and submittal workflows — keep every project detail on schedule.

Custom-Built Gantt Scheduling

A purpose-built Gantt chart with auto-cascading dates, predecessor/successor relationships, and MS Project import. Drag to adjust, and watch downstream tasks update automatically.

  • Auto-cascade date logic
  • Predecessor/successor links
  • MS Project import/export
  • Drag-and-drop scheduling
  • Critical path highlighting
Gantt Chart

Cross-Module Calendar

One calendar that aggregates deadlines from every module — RFI due dates, submittal deadlines, inspection dates, meeting schedules. Sync with Google Calendar for mobile access.

  • Aggregates all module deadlines
  • Google Calendar sync
  • Color-coded by module
  • Day/week/month views
Project Calendar

RFI Tracking & Plan Placement

Create, assign, and track RFIs through their full lifecycle. Pin RFIs directly to plan sheet locations so reviewers see exactly what you're asking about — no more guessing from written descriptions.

  • Full lifecycle tracking
  • Pin to plan sheet locations
  • Assignee and due date management
  • Public share links for responses
RFI Tracker

Submittal Workflow Management

Manage the submittal process from creation through review and approval. Track spec sections, responsible parties, and review cycles. Generate shareable links for consultant review.

  • Multi-step review workflow
  • Spec section tracking
  • Revision management
  • Public share links for reviewers
