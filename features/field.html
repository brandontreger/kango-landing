Field Operations

Documents, plan markups, daily reports, photos, and safety permits — everything your field team needs, accessible from any device.

Organized Document Storage

Upload, categorize, and share project documents with your team. Track revisions, control access, and ensure everyone has the latest version.

  • Category-based organization
  • Revision tracking
  • Access controls
  • Bulk upload support
Document Manager

Fieldwire-Style Plan Markups

Upload plan sheets and mark them up directly in the browser. Add pins, measurements, text annotations, and hyperlinks. Pin RFIs and photos directly to plan locations for full spatial context.

  • Multi-layer markup tools
  • Pin RFIs to plan locations
  • Measurement and annotation tools
  • Revision comparison
Plan Markups

Daily Reports with Auto-Weather

Log daily activities, workforce counts, equipment usage, and weather conditions. Weather data auto-fetches based on project location — no manual entry needed.

  • Automatic weather data
  • Workforce and equipment tracking
  • Activity logging by trade
  • PDF export and distribution
Daily Report

Photo Documentation

Capture and organize project photos with automatic timestamps. Tag photos by location, category, or trade. Link photos to daily reports, RFIs, or punch list items.

  • Auto-timestamped uploads
  • Category and location tagging
  • Link to other modules
  • Gallery and list views
Photo Gallery

Safety Compliance Forms

Generate hot work permits with required fields and approval workflows. Maintain dust logs for regulatory compliance on renovation and demolition projects.

  • Hot work permit templates
  • Approval routing
  • Dust log tracking
  • Compliance documentation
Hot Work Permit
